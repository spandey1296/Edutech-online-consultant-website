NSS EVENTS


NSS DAY
24thSeptember

Inspirational videos were shown on this day and the importance of NSS in our life.This day is of great importance in the life of the NSS volunteer.


BLOOD DONATION


NSS organises BLOOD DONATION in the campus which helped various hospitals receive 500 units of blood everytime and this camp is organised 4 times in a year i.e. 2 per semester.As we know the importance of blood donation in our life, keeping this in mind NSS plays a great role i organising such camps.


CLOTH DONATION

This event is done in nearby villages and provide the clothes and other things like slippers, Blanket, etc to the Needy people. Many faculty members are also involved in this activity.


RALLIES


NSS orgaines rallies on various topics like Swachh Bharat, Drug De-Addiction, Women Empowerment,etc. to create awareness among people.


National Days
26thJanuary
15thAugust

Nukkad Nataks(Street Play) is performed by NSS Volunteers.
Also these plays are performed in various villages to create awareness among people regarding surroundings and many other issues.


SHRAMDAAN


Shramdaan is Basically done to create the awareness about Swachhta. In this all the NSS Volunteers go to nearby villages and clean the surroundings making everyone aware to keep environment clean and green.
It is done inside as well as outside the campus. s


Tree Plantation

This event is done in campus as well as nearby villages. Trees are planted in nearby surroundings to keep the environment neat and clean.
This is also done in nearby villages so as keep the people aware of greenery.


Orphanage, Old home Visits


NSS Volunteers also visit orphange, Old Age HOme, Handicap Home,etc. where we interact with everyone. Various activities and gifts are given to everyone. This gives peace to mind.
